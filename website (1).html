How X-Carbon Works

The X-Carbon device attaches to a vehicle exhaust. A high-temperature stainless steel mesh captures carbon soot particles. The captured carbon is purified and processed to create ink pigment. This pigment is then used to produce writing ink for pens and markers.

Environmental Impact

Vehicle exhaust produces harmful particulate pollution. X-Carbon transforms this pollution into a useful resource. By converting carbon soot into ink, the device demonstrates how waste emissions can be reused for sustainable products.
